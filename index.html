Academic & Personal Projects

Audio Forensics Framework (PyTorch, WavLM, Torchaudio, Librosa)

Capstone project in audio forensics covering speaker profiling, deepfake detection, and environmental sound classification. Used WavLM for speaker attributes and deepfake detection, along with a CRNN model for environmental sound classification. Models were trained and evaluated on Common Voice, RAVDESS, ASVspoof, and ESC-50.

Talk To Philosophers (RAG, LLM, FAISS, Streamlit)

A local RAG-based Streamlit app for chatting with philosophers such as Marcus Aurelius and René Descartes. It retrieves relevant passages with FAISS and generates grounded responses in each philosopher’s style. (code)

Bangla Abstractive Summarizer (PyTorch, Transformers, Hugging Face, Gradio)

Fine-tuned mT5-small on the XL-Sum Bangla dataset for abstractive Bengali text summarization. Deployed it on Hugging Face with an interactive Gradio demo. (demo)

Football Player Similarity Search (Similarity Search, PCA, KNN)

Data-driven system to find statistically similar football players using embedding-based similarity search over player performance metrics. (code)
