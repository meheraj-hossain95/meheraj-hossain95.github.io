Audio Forensics Framework

PyTorch WavLM Torchaudio Librosa

An audio forensics framework as part of a capstone project, covering speaker profiling, deepfake detection, and environmental sound classification. WavLM was used for speaker attributes (age, gender, accent, emotion) and deepfake detection, along with a CRNN model for environmental sound classification. The models were trained and evaluated on Common Voice, RAVDESS, ASVspoof, and ESC-50.
